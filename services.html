Production

Ebey glass and aluminium’s focus is to embrace technology, and improve the fabrication process through the use of technology. Ebey glass and aluminium’s  has a computer operated saw that optimises the cutting of the aluminium, and a two stage CNC machine which ensures the quality of the joinery is on point. Our team of skilled craftsman provide real time updated information of all stages of the manufacture of joinery from acceptance, measure, manufacture, and delivery. This provides us with a unique ability to answer the questions such as what stage the joinery is at, and also monitor delivery.

Window replacement and repairs

If required, we can remove your existing steel or timber frames with a building team, and replace them with aluminium windows and doors and re- plaster the reveals.

 

Aluminium sliding windows

 

Aluminium sliding windows are ideal for openings that do not have space for opening windows projecting from the wall, and allows for maximum ventilation. Our sliding windows are somewhat the same design as our sliding doors, sliding horizontally within the outer frame. An option of two, three or four panels are available, depending on the width of the opening.

About us

Building on Service, Quality & Innovation

 

Ebey glass and aluminium is a 100% black owned South African company that is  aimed at providing the ultimate services for our customers. We produce to the exacting specifications, we help to create and design door and window joinery for residential projects using the latest in aluminium joinery technology.

 

To achieve our high standard we use precision  Engineering in the form of Electronic Cutting and Machining equipment to give your joinery an accurate perfect finish every time, custom made your unique requirements, design, lifestyle and let us not forget your budget.

 

From start to finish, we aim to exceed your expectations; at Ebey glass and aluminium we have the experience and expertise to assist you and our difference is clear. Through specific customer focused solutions and our sense of pride, our team of experts will work closely with you in order to understand and meet all your particular requirements.

 

Our timely management is renowned and  With an aluminium security door, window frames and window glazing from Ebey Windows and Doors your home is secure, stylish and energy efficient.
